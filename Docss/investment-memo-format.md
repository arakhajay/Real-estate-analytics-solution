# ASA Investment Memo Format

## 1. Executive Summary
**Verdict:** [Undervalued / Overvalued / Fair]  
**Confidence Score:** [High/Medium/Low]

Briefly summarize the investment opportunity in 2-3 sentences. Mention the Listing Price vs. AI Value delta.

## 2. Key Investment Drivers (The "Why")
| Driver | Impact | details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Zoning & Infrastructure** | [Positive/Negative] | Brief detail on recent changes (e.g., new subway line, rezoning). |
| **Market Momentum** | [Hot/Cold] | Avg days on market trend or price growth in the area. |
| **Amenities** | [High/Low] | Proximity to key lifestyle anchors (Whole Foods, Equinox, Transit). |

## 3. Risk Factors
*   **Regulatory:** [e.g. Rent Control risk]
*   **Market:** [e.g. Oversupply of 1-beds]

## 4. Financial Snapshot
*   **Asking Rent:** $[Amount]
*   **AI Fair Rent:** $[Amount]
*   **Proj. Yield:** [X%] (Estimate based on standard expenses)

## 5. Recommendation
Final actionable advice for the investment committee (e.g., "Proceed with offer at $3,600").
