# Beekin Business Solutions Replication & Enhancement

## 1. Valuation & Underwriting (Predictive AI)
**Concept:** "Ebby" / "LeaseMax" Equivalent.
**Goal:** Automate the valuation of rental assets with higher accuracy than human underwriters using traditional spreadsheets.
**Features:**
- **Instant Rent Estimate:** Input an address, get a predicted rent range based on 50+ variables (location, amenities, walk score).
- **Investment Grader:** Score a property's investment potential (Cap Rate Prediction, ROI forecast).
- **Comparison Engine:** Automatically find "true" comparables using similarity vector search, not just "same radius".

## 2. Revenue Management (Dynamic Pricing)
**Concept:** Airline-style pricing for apartments.
**Goal:** Maximize revenue by balancing occupancy and rent price.
**Features:**
- **Dynamic Pricing Model:** Suggest daily optimal prices based on supply/demand, seasonality, and competitor moves.
- **Concession Optimizer:** Recommend *when* to offer "1 month free" to maximize long-term Net Effective Rent (NER).
- **Renewal Calculator:** optimal renewal increase % that maximizes revenue without triggering move-outs.

## 3. Resident Retention (Behavioral AI)
**Concept:** "Wilson" Equivalent.
**Goal:** Stop tenant churn before it happens.
**Features:**
- **Churn Risk Score:** A probability score (0-100%) for every tenant indicating likelihood to move out.
- **Engagement Triggers:** Alerts to property managers: "Unit 305 is at risk. Send a gym pass or check maintenance requests."
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Scan maintenance tickets and emails to gauge tenant happiness.

## 4. Enhanced GenAI Solutions (New Features for ASA)
**Goal:** Go beyond spreadsheets to conversational interfaces.
- **Lease Copilot:** A chatbot that answers questions about legal lease terms for tenants and landlords.
- **Marketing Auto-Pilot:** Generate SEO-optimized listings and social media posts automatically from property photos and raw data.
- **Visual Staging:** Use GenAI to "virtually stage" empty units in photos to attract different demographics (e.g., "Bohemian" vs "Minimalist").
